Review some points about MS data

  • The continuous workflow of separating-separating-separating!

  • Three dimensions: Retention time of analyser elution - M/Z - Measurement of Intensity

Spectrum is the unit of mass spectrometry data

TIC (total ion chromatogram) plot: The intensities of all ions in each mass spectrometer scan (within a given time period) are summed and plotted as a function of time. Base peak chromatogram plot: The intensity of the base peak (the most intense ion in each mass spectrum) is plotted as a function of time. (Note: do not confuse the base peak with the baseline, which represents the constant signal from the background level of the instrument.) XIC or EIC plot: The intensity of a specific analyte at a chosen m/z value (either total or base peak intensity) is isolated and plotted as a function of time.
